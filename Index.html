Who We Are

A Global Food Rescue Movement

OZ Harvest Limited was built on a powerful idea — no good food should go to waste while people go hungry anywhere in the world. We bridge the gap between surplus and need, every single day, across every continent.

We collect quality surplus food from supermarkets, restaurants, hotels, airlines, cafes, and farms — then deliver it free of charge to charities and communities supporting vulnerable people worldwide.

We are not just a charity. We are a worldwide movement fighting food waste and hunger simultaneously. Your support keeps us moving globally.

Global Reach

We Are Worldwide

OZ Harvest Limited operates across 57 countries, partnering with local charities, governments, and communities to tackle food insecurity and food waste at a truly global scale.

From urban cities to remote communities, our food rescue network reaches the people who need it most — wherever they are in the world. With 5,182 volunteers driving our mission every single day.

Our worldwide model is built on one simple principle: connect surplus food to people in need. Fast. Free. With dignity. Everywhere.
